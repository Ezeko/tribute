WARREN BUFFET

  • Buffet was born in Omaha, Nebraska on August 30, 1930.
  • Howard and Leila Buffet are the parents of Warren Buffet.
  • OCCUPATION: Buffet is an Investor, a business magnate, a speaker and also a philantropist.
  • He is known to be the Leader of Berkshire Hathaway with Charlie Munger
  • Buffet was married to Susan Thompson who died in 2004. He then married Astrid Menks in 2006
  • Buffet is known to be one of the world's richest man with net worth of US$82 billion.
  • Buffet is the chairman and CEO of Berkshire.
  • Buffet has three children, Susan, Howard and Peter.
  • Buffet graduated from University of Nebraska at the age of 19.
  • He also attended New York Institute of Finance to focus his economics background.
